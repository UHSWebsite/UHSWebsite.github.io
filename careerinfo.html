About

Planning for the future requires some thorough research and planning. Those students who are considering education or training beyond high school can save themselves time and money by developing specific career goals. Students are encouraged to visit the Career Center early and often – 10th grade is not too soon to begin career exploration and planning.

The Utica High School Career Center is located on the second floor in the Career/Counseling Center. Mrs. Wynn is available between 7:00 a.m. – 2:30 p.m. to help students with all career planning activities, and future careers. You may also call her at 586-797-2366.
