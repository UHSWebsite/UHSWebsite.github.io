College Presentations at Utica High School
College presentations have begun at Utica High School and will continue throughout the fall. Interested students can click here to sign up for a college presentation.

College Applications
It is recommended that students complete their college applications by Thanksgiving. Some colleges take 8-12 weeks to notify students of their acceptance. If you apply online, be sure to go through Parchment in order to send your high school transcript to interested colleges. If you have any questions, please stop inside the Counseling Office.

All Colleges Offer Campus Visits
If a college you are interested in is not listed, go to their website and check into it's upcoming open house and scheduled campus visits. Remember, we recommend a visit to a campus to help you decide your future.
