Take Classes That Might Challenge You In Your Senior Year!

While some may want to take the easy way out during their senior year, you may want to challenge yourself as you head off towards post-secondary schooling. Some challenging classes offered at Utica High School are:

  • AP Statistics

  • AP Calculus AB/BC

  • AP Biology

  • AP Capstone Seminar

  • AP European History

  • AP Literature and Composition
